Attributes

Attribues are special words used **inside the opening tag* of an element to **provide extra imformation** about that element. They always follow a name=value structure.

Example: The Anchor Tag

This tag is used to create hyperlinks. The mos common attribue is href, which indicates the link's destincation URL.
